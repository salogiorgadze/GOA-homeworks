Unordered List

An unordered list in HTML is used to present a collection of items where the order of the items is not significant. These lists are typically displayed with bullet points by default in web browsers.

  • COFFEE
    • Cappuccino
    • Late
    • Americano
  • TEA
    • Green Tea
    • Black Tea
    • Red Berry Tea

Ordered List

An ordered list in HTML is used to display a list of items in a specific, sequential order. This order is typically indicated by numbers or letters.

Coffee

  1. Cappuccino
  2. Late
  3. Americano

Tea

  1. Green Tea
  2. Black Tea
  3. Red Berry Tea

Milkshake

  1. Vanilla Milkshake
  2. Chocolate Milkshake
  3. Banana Milkshake
