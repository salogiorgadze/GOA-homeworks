ESSENTIAL BEAUTY FORMULATIONS FROM CALIFORNIA. FOR THE SKIN AND SENSES.

On the coast of Southern California, known for its perfect weather, ease of living and natural beauty, two friends with a common obsession for skincare and makeup (and over 20 years of collective experience in the industry) set off to reimagine what their daily routines could look and feel like.

Lauren as a busy mother, and Marianna with her jet-set schedule, identified a need for beauty that did more without overdoing it.

Their first product, Jet Lag Mask, embodied efficacy and simplicity.
It was versatile.
It was clean.
It was multi-purpose.
It sold out instantly.

Today the vision has grown into a carefully curated assortment of powerful-yet-gentle skincare products and hybrid makeup to make routines feel effortless and skin look luminous so every day feels like a Summer Friday.
